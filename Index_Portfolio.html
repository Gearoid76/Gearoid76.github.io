Enviorentmental Game

Low resolution

This is a plan of an application, On a mobile device, which can be used in schools to educate primary school children in the advantages of recycling. This was a part of our course in Haaga-Helia. To come up with an idea on how to educate children and make it fun in the process. Here is what we came up with.

STORY: 2125: The world has become a jungle again, the third "elemental" war has either exiled the upper classes to Earth 2.0, left the lower classes, solders and criminals to die here.

What they didn't expect is that Earth as become an Eden once more, But there are dangers. Animals that are still sufferoing from the illeffects of the rubbish left behind. Choking them. Causing them pain. Turning them into monsters.
